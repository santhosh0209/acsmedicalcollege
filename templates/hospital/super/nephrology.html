Nephrology

Overview

One of the key Departments of the Hospital and a brainchild of the Chairman Sri A.C. Shanmugam, the Dialysis Unit was set up with the aim of serving the poor and weaker sections of society. Established in July 2011 with 5 Dialysis machines, today the facility is fully equipped with 10 more machines, adequate support staff and also has a facility for isolating and treating HIV patients. This has become a boon for the rural population around Bengaluru.
